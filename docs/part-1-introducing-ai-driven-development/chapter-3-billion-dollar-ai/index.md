# Chapter 3: How to Make a Billion Dollars in the AI Era

## The Billion Dollar Question

How can individual developers or small teams compete in markets dominated by well-funded corporations?

## Snakes and Ladders Framework

The AI era creates "ladders" (accelerators) and "snakes" (pitfalls):

**Ladders:**
- Vertical specialization in niche markets
- Domain expertise combined with AI tools
- Speed to market with AI-assisted development

**Snakes:**
- Competing directly with AI giants
- Building horizontal platforms without differentiation
- Ignoring the AI transformation

## The PPP Strategy

**Piggyback Protocol Pivot:**
1. **Piggyback**: Build on existing platforms and ecosystems
2. **Protocol**: Establish standards others depend on
3. **Pivot**: Expand once you've gained market position

## Super Orchestrators

The new role combining:
- Domain expertise
- AI orchestration skills
- System design thinking
- Business strategy

## Vertical Intelligence

Why vertical intelligence matters more than reusable code:
- Deep domain knowledge creates moats
- AI makes implementation commodity
- Specialized solutions command premium pricing
- Harder for generalists to compete

## Three Requirements

1. **Domain Expertise**: Deep knowledge in a specific field
2. **AI Proficiency**: Ability to leverage AI tools effectively
3. **Execution Speed**: Rapid iteration and deployment
